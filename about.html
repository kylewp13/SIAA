About SIAA

We are a 501(c)3 non-profit, located at 210 West 7th Street in the heart of Springfield Jacksonville, Florida. We have deep historical roots in the community that originate from the Springfield Improvement Association and Woman's Club that founded 1904. We later consolidated with the Springfield Heritage Education Center when we acquired many precious artifacts of our neighborhood such as the 1985 Property Survey Records.

We are an all volunteer based organization working to enhance the quality of life in our community, preserving its history, and educating it while working to connect our neighbors socially.
